About us

Company Overview

Abanolwazi Enterprise (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black-owned and managed engineering company. Founded in June 2019 by professional practitioners with extensive collective experience in various industries within different engineering and construction fields, Abanolwazi Enterprise (Pty) Ltd provides qualitative engineering expertise and a complete suite of construction services. We deliver projects that fully meet our client's expectations in the mining, power, port, infrastructure, fast-moving consumer goods, and industrial sectors. Our flexible contracting options are tailored to small to mega-sized projects, covering technical audits, studies, EPCM services, and turnkey solutions. We are committed to executing projects on schedule, to world-class quality standards, with tight control over costs and a zero-harm approach to our people and the environment.
